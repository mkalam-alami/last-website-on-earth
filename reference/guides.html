Unlocking your tablet due to failing upgrades

Because of the Downfall, the Teplin servers on Earth are all out of order, and your Teplin tablets will not be able to upgrade their system versions.

A securiy in place makes the tablets lock themselves after spending 3 years without any upgrade patches. To resolve this, follow the procedure below:

  • Turn off the tablet
  • Hold down the top left and bottom right corners of the glass for about 20 seconds
  • A "BIOS" screen will appear and ask for a "private key"
  • Hover your glass over a screen diplaying this key
  • In the menu, rotate to the "Updates" nugget, pick it and unswipe any alerts
  • Disable the automatic updates by unfilling the matching box
  • Pick save and restart the tablet
